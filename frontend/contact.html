Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a civic issue?

To report an issue, create an account on PublicVision, then click on the "Add Complaint" button. Fill in the details about the issue, upload photos if available, mark the location on the map, and submit your complaint.

How can my municipality partner with PublicVision?

We offer partnership programs for municipalities that want to integrate our platform with their existing systems. Please contact our partnerships team at partners@publicvision.com or fill out the contact form with "Partnership Opportunities" as the inquiry type.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We only share necessary information with municipal authorities to resolve reported issues. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

How long does it typically take for issues to be resolved?

Resolution times vary depending on the type and severity of the issue, as well as the responsiveness of the local authorities. Our statistics show that the average resolution time across all categories is approximately 8-10 days, with urgent issues often addressed more quickly.

How can I support PublicVision's mission?

You can support our mission by actively using the platform, reporting issues in your community, engaging with existing reports through upvotes and comments, and spreading the word about PublicVision to friends, family, and local community groups.

Have more questions? Check out our complete FAQ page or contact us directly.
